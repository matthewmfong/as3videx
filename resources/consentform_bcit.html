Project Title

ViDeX, a robust video player that supports learning from personalized video

Investigators

Matthew Fong, Xueqin Zhang, Gregor Miller, Sidney Fels. This research is to be completed for a graduate thesis under the supervision of Dr. Sidney Fels (604-822-5338).

Project Purpose and Procedures

This project is designed to understand users� navigation behaviour through a video space and to investigate how students interact with video in their coursework. Participants will be given an application to watch their course videos. This application will provide tools that will aid participants in studying from the videos provided by their instructors. Participation in this study is risk free and will provide users with tools to watch their course videos. Participants will be asked to use the application for the duration of the term. No monetary compensation will be offered.

Confidentiality

The data gathered from this application will be anonymised for the purposes of subsequent analysis, preserving the participant�s confidentiality. No identifiable information will be kept. All data from individual participants will be coded so that their anonymity will be protected in any project reports and presentations that result from this work.
